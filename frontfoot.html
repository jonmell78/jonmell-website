"Let me check with my manager" is costing you more than you think.

Every time a CSM says those six words, your customer's expectations rise, your team burns time on an internal conversation, and the answer is usually still no.

The problem

It is less about the people, more the situation they are put in.

Multiply that across hundreds of accounts and you have a structural drag on capacity, retention, and customer satisfaction.

Difficult customer conversations require policy knowledge, commercial judgment, and the confidence to hold a position clearly and quickly. Without the right support, even experienced CSMs escalate when they should not have to. Expectations rise. The answer is still no. And three people's time has been spent to get there.

Most AI tools respond by removing the human from the loop entirely. Frontfoot takes a different view.

What we do

We build custom AI agents that give your team the answer, the tone, and the confidence to respond without escalating.

Each agent is built around your commercial context, your policy, and your customer base. Less a platform you configure yourself, more a working system delivered and maintained by people who have led CS organisations across B2B SaaS businesses in Europe, the US, and APAC.

The goal is straightforward: to raise every response to the standard of your best one.
