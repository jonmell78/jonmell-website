How I work

The approach

Every organisation is different — there is no one-size-fits-all playbook. But there is one constant: a clear understanding of the value you create for your customers is the north star that guides everything else.

01

Start with customer value

Before anything else, I work to understand what value genuinely looks like for your customers. That shapes every decision that follows — the model, the metrics, the team structure, the playbooks.

02

Prioritise ruthlessly

Not everything needs fixing at once. I identify the highest-leverage interventions and sequence them in a way the team can actually execute alongside BAU.

03

Build for ownership

Everything I build is designed to be owned and operated by your team. I don't create dependencies — I build capability and then hand over cleanly.

04

Stay commercially grounded

Customer Success only matters if it moves the numbers. Every initiative is tied to retention, expansion, or cost-to-serve outcomes — not just activity metrics.
